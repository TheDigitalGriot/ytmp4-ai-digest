How many videos will you typically compare?

This affects layout decisions — side-by-side vs stacked, timeline density, etc.

A

2-3 Videos

Focused deep comparison. Side-by-side layout works well. E.g., comparing two creators' takes on the same announcement.

B

4-6 Videos

Broader coverage of a topic. Needs a more structured layout — tabbed or card-based. E.g., surveying multiple reactions to a major AI release.

C

Flexible (2-10+)

Design should scale from 2 to many. Overview dashboard that can zoom into individual videos or pairs. Handles both quick comparisons and comprehensive topic surveys.
